Our Mission

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all the games really free? ▼
Yes, 100%. Every game on JeyGames is completely free to play. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no in-app purchases on our end. We make money through non-intrusive ads which keep the site running for everyone.
Will these games work at my school? ▼
Most of our games are served through alternative URLs specifically to bypass school filters. However, some networks are stricter than others. If a game doesn't load, try a different one — most will work on standard school networks.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
Not at all! JeyGames requires zero sign-ups. Just visit the site and start playing instantly. The only exception is if you want to apply to our Earn program, where we collect your details to send payments.
How often are new games added? ▼
We're constantly adding new games — typically several per week. We monitor what's trending and what our community wants. If you have a suggestion for a game to add, feel free to reach out!
Are the games safe for kids? ▼
We curate games to be fun and generally appropriate. Some games like Five Nights at Freddy's have mild horror elements, but nothing explicit or adult. We aim for a teen-friendly catalogue that's still exciting and engaging.
Can I suggest a game? ▼
Absolutely! We love hearing what games our community wants. Reach out through our contact info below and tell us what you'd like to see added. If it's a good fit, we'll add it ASAP.
How does the Earn programme work? ▼
Content creators can apply to post videos featuring JeyGames content. Once your video reaches a set view milestone, you get paid. Head to the Earn page to apply and see full payout details.
