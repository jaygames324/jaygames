Content Rules

Required

  • Must feature at least one JeyGames game prominently
  • Link to JeyGames in description or bio
  • Include #JeyGames in the caption/tags
  • Video must be at least 60 seconds long
  • Content must be your own original footage
  • Views must be organic (no purchased views)
  • Submit proof (screenshot + link) to claim payout

Not Allowed

  • Paid/bought views or engagement
  • Re-uploads of other people's content
  • Misleading thumbnails or clickbait lies
  • Inappropriate, harmful or adult content
  • Multiple channels claiming the same video
  • Content that promotes illegal activity
  • Using automated bots to inflate stats

Game Requirements

Required

  • Must be an original game you made or own rights to
  • Must run in the browser (HTML5 / iframe embeddable)
  • Must have a working playable link we can test
  • Must be appropriate for all ages (or clearly labelled)
  • Must have a thumbnail/cover image (min 512x384)
  • Submit play count proof (screenshot) to claim payout

Not Allowed

  • Games you don't own or have permission to publish
  • Adult, violent or inappropriate content
  • Games that are broken, crash or don't load
  • Fake or inflated play counts
  • Games already listed on JeyGames
  • Clones or direct copies of existing games
